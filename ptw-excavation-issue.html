ISSUE EXCAVATION WORK PERMIT

  1. This permit is required by W.B.F.R., 1968 Rule 64, Sch XVII, Part-II, Para 20.
  2. This permit shall be issued by Responsible work In-charge head / designee. This permit is valid for the time mentioned on the permit.
  3. This permit shall be closed after completion / non-completion of work. (At the end of the day)
  4. In case of fire alarm / siren, all work must immediately be stopped.
  5. Job should be stopped in case of poor illumination level at the job site.
  6. For open area activity, job should be stopped in case of rain and storm.
